LA BARAKA

BAR-B&B LE CHABLE

Why Us, Why Here

La Baraka is a charming hotel-lounge bar in the heart of Le Châble, a 5-minute walk from the cable cars / gondolas.
Seb and Lolo are looking forward to welcoming you to the Baraka.
See you soon!

Fun

Discover the incredible range of activities in Verbier, on foot, snowshoeing, snowboarding, skiing, telemarking , cycling or flying up in the air.

Relax

Treat yourself to a relaxing massage, a yoga session, then a delicious fondue with a glass of local wine.

Can work too

A coworking area is also available every evening from 5 pm.


Landscape & Lifestyle

Hotel

La Baraka is a guest house at Le Châble, in an old house. Its rooms are entirely quiet and comfortable. The hotel is close to the village and the ski lifts.
